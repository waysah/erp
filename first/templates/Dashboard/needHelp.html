LOGIN PAGE

The System automatically redirects to login page.The login credentials are supposed to be arquired from Admin.


System Configuration

Navigate to the right upper corner of the Dashboard as indicated below.Check the drop down menu and click on settings

Profile Settings

Navigate to upper right corner and click on Admin Profile,check on drop down and Click or tap on Account Settings.Configure As Per Your Details.More functionalities will appear in coming version

Profile View

Navigate to upper right corner and click on Admin Profile,check on drop down and click on profile.

Profile View

Navigate to view profile alt2 navigate sidebar and click on profile.

Sign out

Navigate to upper right corner and click on Admin Profile,check on drop down and click on sign out.

System usage

To kick start the system functionality everything starts at Adding department first.

Other functionalities entirely are based on department since to add an employee or job department is a key requirement.

To add Department head on to sidebar and navigate to Department click to Add Department in the Drop down menu

Job Types

Then Add diffrents kind of jobs.Navigate to sidebar,click on payroll then select commission templates.

Add Employee

This should be third task consecutively.To add an employee navigate to sidebar then on Employees, under drop down click on Add Employee

Daily Task

Navigate to upper right side of the dashboard.Click to message like icon.A drop down will appear.Click on Add Daily Task
